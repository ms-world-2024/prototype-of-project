🌾 Organic Farming Methods

🍂

Composting

Convert organic waste into nutrient-rich compost. Mix green materials (kitchen scraps, fresh grass) with brown materials (dry leaves, paper) in 3:1 ratio.

Process: Layer materials, maintain moisture, turn every 2 weeks. Ready in 3-6 months.

🐛

Biological Pest Control

Use beneficial insects, birds, and natural predators to control harmful pests without chemicals.

Examples: Ladybugs for aphids, neem oil spray, companion planting with marigolds.

🔄

Crop Rotation

Rotate different crop families to maintain soil fertility and break pest cycles naturally.

Pattern: Legumes → Cereals → Root crops → Leafy vegetables

🌿

Green Manuring

Grow nitrogen-fixing plants like dhaincha, sunhemp, and cowpea, then incorporate into soil.

Benefits: Adds 40-60 kg nitrogen per hectare, improves soil structure.

🐄

Organic Fertilizers

Use farmyard manure, vermicompost, bone meal, and organic liquid fertilizers for plant nutrition.

Application: 10-15 tonnes FYM per hectare, 2-3 tonnes vermicompost.

💧

Water Management

Efficient irrigation methods like drip irrigation and mulching to conserve water and prevent soil erosion.

Mulching: Use organic materials to retain moisture and suppress weeds.

Inspection
